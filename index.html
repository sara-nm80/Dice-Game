How to Play Show

To play the game, click on the "Roll Dice" button.
Both the player and the opponent will roll two dice three times.
The scores are calculated based on the dice rolls.
After three rounds, the total scores are compared to determine the winner.

Rules Show

1. If any of the player's two dice comes up as a 1, the score for that round is 0.
2. If the player rolls a pair of the same numbers, the score is the total of the two dice times 2.
3. Any other combination of dice results in the score being the total value of the two dice.
